Beanstalk

  • Superminimal queue. Basically a toy... but what a toy!
  • PeepCode uses it for spam checking
  • An "Extended Subset" of standard message queues
  • Super simple to install. Not internally scalable--must be scaled in architecture.
  • Super simple to use. Not terribly robust.
  • No persistence; Very fast
  • No topics; it's just a series of tubes (We love you, Senator Stevens)
  • Tubes aren't even necessary -- default tube
  • Work queue only
  • Allows burying, expiration and priority
